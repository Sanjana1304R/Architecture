FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Bauen. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to us directly.

How long does a typical project take? +

Timelines vary depending on scope. A residential project typically takes 4–8 months from concept to completion, while larger commercial projects may span 12–24 months.

Do you handle construction management? +

Yes. Our Premium and Bespoke plans include full construction oversight, ensuring the built result matches our designs precisely.

What is your design process? +

We follow a four-phase approach: Discovery, Concept Design, Development, and Delivery. Each phase includes client reviews and approvals before proceeding.

Do you work internationally? +

Absolutely. We have completed projects across 18 countries and maintain partnerships with local contractors and regulatory specialists worldwide.
